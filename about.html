Our Timeline

  • Teaming Up

    last summer

    We first met on our Facebook group and found same interest and appropriate level of knowledge about topic and decided to team up and help each others growth by solving common issues, discussion, and learning from teaching.

  • Started Helping

    We discovered that Facebook group where we met having lots of people sicking out for knowledge and expecting help from any person possible, but people are just trolling them as much as possible. So we decided to help them out and established our first group The Hacker's Forum, which later become an interactive group for discussions. Currently we are having 30k+ peoples and we are happy for having such a great community. After achieving this success, we thought upon another group where all the tutorials were to be posted, which finally is The Hacker's Point after people started copying our posts to other groups without giving a single credit, the decision was taken to kick out those people and move to new policies. Hence as s welcome note, if you want to join this group try to be loyal member on the Forum.

    • The Hacker's Forum
    • The Hacker's Point

  • Final Establishment

    January 2018

    After such huge response from our Facebook groups we finally decided to establish an organization which took almost 2 months to work on plan and execute it perfectly. From here we have some different goals want to have some name and fame and gather more geeks around us.

  • Further Growth

    Whilst the formation and shaping of CodeSploit we never did forget our old goals. The CodeSploit Facebook page was developed where all the official updates and posts are to be uploaded, making our old groups sync under the flow of CodeSploit. While making posts on our tutorial forum, we found many people requesting video tutorials as some of them having language problem, some just want an good representation and many other requests so we came up with idea of Youtube Channel which is liked and supported by quite a numer of people.
